Welcome to KScribe

A Tool for TS Agents

KScribe is designed to help you efficiently document call case details, organize information, and guide you through call processes seamlessly. Built specifically with Call Center TS agents in mind, this app ensures you spend less time worrying about note-taking and more time focusing on resolving issues.

What does this tool offer?

  • A user-friendly interface to quickly create, view, and manage notes.
  • No more copying a template to start a note on every call. Just press new.
  • Gather all the relevant information and have access to it in one place.
  • Dynamic text areas for notes that grow with your content.
  • Guided prompts to ensure all critical information is captured and troubleshooting is performed during a call.
  • Storage and organization of notes for easy retrieval.

How to Use:

  1. Click the button below to launch the KScribe app in a minimal window. You can resize it to your preference.
  2. Create a new note with each call by clicking "New Note".
  3. Hit "View" to see the resulting note. Copy the note and use it where needed.
  4. Save and manage your notes for future reference or reporting.
